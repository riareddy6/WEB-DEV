My Travel Pet Peeves

Traveling is amazing, but there are always a few things that drive me crazy. Here’s my list.

  • Long flights with no breaks — Sitting still for hours is the worst. I always end up tired and stiff, not to mention it doesn't help the nausea.
  • No extra snacks on flights — Why do they give you one tiny cookie and call it a day? Even that's a stretch sometimes.
  • Crying babies — I get it, babies cry, but it’s hard to keep your sanity.
  • Booking rides abroad — Trying to find a good taxi or rideshare in another country can be very annoying.
  • Hotel service — When check-in takes forever or the room isn’t ready, and there's nowhere to wait.
  • Overcrowded tourist spots — Some places look peaceful online but are packed in real life.
  • Different prices for tourists — It’s weird when you know locals are paying less for the same thing.
  • Currency conversion — I always feel like I’m messing up the math or getting scammed.
  • Bad Wi-Fi — When the hotel says “free Wi-Fi” but it barely loads Google Maps. Or your cellular data couldn't even save your life... quite literally.
