My Travel Advice

Here’s some stuff I’ve learned from traveling — little things that make trips go smoother and way less stressful.

  • Pack light — You don’t need half the stuff you think you do.
  • Keep your essentials close — Passport, charger, cash, headphones. Always.
  • Download offline maps — Wi-Fi dies when you need it most. Save addresses on your phone too.
  • Drink water — Especially on long flights or hot days. But be careful, watch out for water quality!
  • Learn a few local words — Even a simple “thank you” or “hello” helps a lot.
  • Try local food — Don’t just look for the nearest McDonald’s.
  • Respect the culture — If locals do something a certain way, go with it.
  • Always have backup cash — ATMs and cards love to stop working at the worst times.
  • Take photos, but live it too — Don’t spend your whole trip staring through a screen.
  • Adapt when plans change — Travel never goes 100% smoothly. Just roll with it.
