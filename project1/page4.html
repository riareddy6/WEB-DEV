More Bucket List Spots

Spain

Spain is full of culture, art, and amazing food. I’d go to Barcelona, watch soccer, explore the beautiful streets and beaches, and go shopping.

Portugal

Portugal has beautiful coastlines and colorful streets. I'd like to go to Portugal and Spain in one trip.

Germany

Germany has fairytale castles and lots of history. I’d love to see Munich and explore the famous Neuschwanstein Castle.

China

China has much more technological innovation and diversity than I thought. In recent years, I have realized that China is a very interesting place to visit. Chengdu, Shanghai, Beijing, Huangzhou, the snowy northen cities, Buddhist cities at its Tibet borders, and Hong Kong are all places I'd go to.

Thailand

Thailand is known for its temples, beaches, cuisine, and friendly people. I’d want to visit the beaches and explore around the city.

Rajasthan (Visit Again)

Rajasthan’s royal palaces are stunning, and the food and service are great. I want to revisit Jaipur, Udaipur, and Jodhpur to explore more.
