Dream Destinations: Italy & Greece

These are two of my top dream travel locations. I would love to explore the historical sites, gorgeous views, and try authentic food!

Here are some of the spots I'd like to visit for each location.

Italy

Colosseum, Rome

  • Rome – Colosseum, Trevi Fountain, Vatican City
  • Venice – Gondola rides and St. Mark’s Square
  • Florence – Renaissance art and the Duomo
  • Amalfi Coast – Scenic cliffside beaches
  • Milan – Fashion, shopping, and architecture

Greece

Santorini, Greece

  • Athens – The Acropolis and Parthenon
  • Santorini – Blue-domed houses and sunsets
  • Mykonos – Whitewashed streets and beaches
  • Crete – Beautiful coastlines and ancient ruins
  • Delphi – Historic temples and mountain views
